Welcome to #Geo-Search

#Geo-Search enables you to generate custom search feeds to monitor live tweets. To get started, search for a hashtag, @twitter handle, or generic search tearm in Search area. #Geo-Search only supports 3 feeds at a time. You can remove existing feeds at any time by clicking remove

#Geo-Search also supports search for geo-tagged tweets and location based searching. To conduct a geo-search, navigate to the the Geo-Search section. Enter your search query, the location and radius and select search.
